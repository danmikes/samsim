Insolation

Insolation is the composite curve of three orbital cycles: eccentricity, obliquity, precession. This curve affects average global temperature. In turn global temperature affects absolute global sea-level.

Shoreline-trajectory is the path of shoreline locations through rock-record. This curve records sea-level relative to the continent. Assuming (naively) continent static it represents global sea-level.

Critical question is: How reliable is inference of sea-level from shoreline-trajectory?

This platform addresses precisely that question. How sensitive is inference of sea-level from shoreline-trajectory to sample-rate.
