Simulation

The simulation consists of three steps

  1. generation of insolation-curve
  2. random sampling
  3. evenly spreading
  4. fit

Insolation-curve can be modified by adjusting properties of the three composing signals. This is true-signal.

A chosen number of samples are randomly taken along the insolation-curve. Samples are evenly spread along time to yield apparent-signal

Fit is given as number of apparent-signal baseline-crossings divided by number of true-signal baseline crossings.
